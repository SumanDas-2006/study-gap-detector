#  Study Gap Detector

A smart productivity tool that helps students prepare efficiently under time pressure by identifying study gaps and generating a focused last-minute plan.

---

##  Problem

Students often struggle during exams due to:
- Incomplete syllabus coverage
- Poor time management
- No clear prioritization of topics

This leads to stress and inefficient studying.

---

##  Solution

Study Gap Detector analyzes what you’ve studied vs what’s left and creates a **high-impact study plan**.

---

##  Features

###  Study Gap Detection
- Identifies uncovered or weak topics
- Highlights high-priority areas

###  Last-Minute Planner
- Allocates time based on importance
- Generates “what to study now” plan

###  Smart Prioritization
- Weighs topics based on urgency & coverage
- Reduces decision fatigue

---

##  Tech Stack

- Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript  
- Backend: Node.js / Python (Flask)  
- Logic Engine: Custom prioritization algorithm  

---

##  How It Works

1. Input:
   - Total syllabus
   - Completed topics
   - Time remaining

2. Processing:
   - Detect gaps
   - Assign priority scores
   - Allocate time slots

3. Output:
   - Actionable study plan
